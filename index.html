Survival Clearance

Master the art of survival in a world where every decision matters

Game Overview

Survival Clearance puts your survival skills to the ultimate test. Navigate through challenging environments, manage resources wisely, and overcome increasingly difficult obstacles. Every playthrough offers a unique experience with procedurally generated challenges.

Key Features

  • Dynamic survival mechanics
  • Procedural world generation
  • Advanced AI systems
  • Progressive difficulty system

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Movement
  • Space - Special Action
  • E - Interact
  • Tab - Inventory

Tips

  • Manage your resources carefully
  • Stay alert for environmental hazards
  • Upgrade your abilities strategically
  • Explore to find better equipment
